//Brute Force approach
Example1:
    when a person forget his password containing 4 digits then he tries to find out his password by placing all possible combinations.
    in the worst case it takes 10000 tries to find the perfect combination.
Example2:
    classical example in computer science is the travelling salesman problem.
    suppose a salesman needs to visit 10 cities accross the country he needs to determine the minimum distance he can travel.
    the BruteForse solution is to calculate total distance for every possible routes and then select the shortes one.
